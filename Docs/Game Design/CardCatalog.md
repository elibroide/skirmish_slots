## Actions (7 Cards)

### Assassinate
**Effect:** Kill an enemy with power 5 or greater

---

### Energize
**Effect:** An ally gets +3

---

### Fireball
**Effect:** Choose a slot, deal 2 damage to unit on it and each close unit
**WARNING:** Damages your units too if they're close!

---

### Repositioning
**Effect:** Move a unit to a close slot of same player (switch positions if occupied)

---

### Seed
**Effect:** Give a slot and close slots +1

---

### Strike
**Effect:** Deal 3 damage to a unit

---

### Unsummon
**Effect:** Return a unit to hand
